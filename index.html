Rescue_PI


Problem

  • Problem:
    • Currently, there is no effective way of detecting crime and other conflicts over large areas and distances, such as within a large metropolitan city.
  • Examples of these conflicts include:
    • Gang violence
    • Assault
    • General Distress

Statistics

  • According to Youth.gov, gang-related homicides increased by 13 percent between 2009 and 2012(Source)
  • The study also shows that In Los Angeles and Chicago, the "gang capitals" of the country, gang-related violence accounted for almost half of homicides recorded(Source)

    • (Source)
  • 41.6% of gangs are found in larger cities(Source)
  • Almost 270,000 cases of robbery werreported in 2019 in the US(Source)
  • America received almost 80 million international visitors in 2019 (before the COVID-19 pandemic). Tourists are likely to get lost, so it would make sense for many of them to be worried and stressed. Rescue_PI can aid these lost travellers.(Source)

Solution

  • Rescue_PI utilizes deep learning and image detection to accomplish:
    • Uses an unmanned aerial vehicle as a capable and efficient platform
    • Utilizes machine learning and image detection to identify when people need assistance, or when authorities need to be contacted to break up a fight.
  • Rescue_PI can help identify and address problems and conflicts efficiently within large metropolitan areas

Future Implementation

  • Possible Improvements:
    • Given more time, we could easily train Rescue_PI to detect other accidents, such as
      • Fires
      • Car Crashes
    • After detecting such incidents, Rescue_PI can:
      • Alert emergency medical services
      • Call the authorities
    • We could also fully automate the drone to roam around in a pre-designated pattern to methodically sweep an area to check for people in need.
